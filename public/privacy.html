Privacy Policy

Samsara — Peptide Tracking & Body Composition Analysis

Effective date: April 11, 2026

Samsara is designed with your privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data the app handles, how it is stored, and what control you have over it.

Information We Collect

Samsara may process the following types of information, all of which you provide voluntarily:

  • Dose logs — peptide names, dosages, timestamps, and injection sites
  • Body statistics — weight, measurements, and body composition data
  • Lab results — bloodwork values and related health markers
  • Protocol information — cycle schedules, compound stacks, and notes
  • Body photos — progress images you choose to capture or upload
  • Lab images — photos or screenshots of lab reports

Samsara does not require an account, login, or any form of authentication. No email address, name, or other identifying information is collected.

How Your Data Is Stored

All user data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB. Your data never leaves your device except when you explicitly request AI analysis (see below) or export your data.

  • There is no cloud storage, no remote database, and no server-side persistence.
  • There are no user accounts or authentication systems.
  • Clearing your browser data will permanently delete all app data.

AI Analysis & Photo Processing

When you use the AI analysis features, body photos and lab images are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. Important details about this process:

  • Images are transmitted directly to Anthropic's API over an encrypted connection.
  • Images are not stored on any server after processing is complete.
  • Anthropic processes the data according to their own privacy policy and API terms of service.
  • No images are retained by Samsara on any external server at any time.
  • You are always in control of when and whether to send images for analysis.

Data You Control

You have full ownership of your data at all times:

  • Export — you can export all of your data as a JSON file at any time.
  • Import — you can import previously exported JSON data to restore or transfer your information.
  • Delete — you can clear all app data through the app settings or by clearing your browser's site data.

Because your data lives entirely on your device, no request to a server is needed to access, modify, or delete it.

Third Parties

Samsara does not use:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Advertising networks or ad tracking
  • Tracking pixels, beacons, or fingerprinting
  • Social media integrations or share buttons
  • Any data broker or data-sharing arrangement

The only external service the app communicates with is Anthropic's Claude API, and only when you explicitly initiate an AI analysis request. Samsara does not sell or share your personal data with anyone.

Children's Privacy

Samsara is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children. If you believe a minor has used the app, their data exists only on their local device and can be removed by clearing the browser's site data.

Medical Disclaimer

Samsara is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. AI-generated analysis is not a substitute for professional medical judgment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before making decisions about peptides, supplements, or any health-related protocol.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Because Samsara does not collect email addresses or contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can reach us at privacy@samsara.app.
